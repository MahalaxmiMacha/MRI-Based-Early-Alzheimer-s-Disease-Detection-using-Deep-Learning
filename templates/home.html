Alzheimer Disease Types

Alzheimer's Disease (AD)

A progressive neurological condition causing memory loss, confusion, and personality changes.

Cognitively Normal (CN)

Individuals with no memory problems or impairment.

Early Mild Cognitive Impairment (EMCI)

Mild memory problems that may not affect daily life.

Late Mild Cognitive Impairment (LMCI)

More noticeable symptoms with increased Alzheimer's risk.
